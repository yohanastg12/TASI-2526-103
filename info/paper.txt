Automated Writing Assessment 

Text 

Topic coherence 

Keterkaitan logis antar bagian teks secara keseluruhan — seberapa konsisten dan terhubung topik yang dibahas dalam tulisan. 

“Topic coherence (0.108) in semantic features emerges as the single most important feature, reflecting the central role of logical consistency in critical thinking.” 

Argument density 

Jumlah rata-rata unit argumentasi per 100 karakter teks — mengukur seberapa padat struktur argumen dalam tulisan. 

“The argument density indicator shows that Grade 9 students include an average of 0.38 argumentative units per 100 characters” 

Sentence complexity 

Tingkat kerumitan struktur kalimat — semakin kompleks susunan kalimat, semakin dalam hubungan logis yang dikandungnya. 

“The high importance of syntactic complexity (0.089) indicates the close connection between language expression ability and thinking depth, with complex syntactic structures often carrying more refined logical relationships” 

Dependency depth 

Kedalaman jalur ketergantungan (dependency path) antar kata atau komponen dalam struktur sintaksis kalimat. 

“simsyntactic evaluates syntactic association strength based on dependency path length” 

Causal connectives 

Kata atau frasa penghubung yang menandai hubungan sebab-akibat antar komponen argumen, seperti "reduces", "impairs", "therefore". 

“causal connectives (0.072) play a crucial role in identifying reasoning chains” 

 

“Causal markers detected: 'reduces', 'impairs', 'Therefore', 'can improve” 

Contrast markers 

Penanda linguistik yang menunjukkan pertentangan atau sanggahan, digunakan untuk mengidentifikasi struktur rebuttal dalam tulisan argumentatif. 

“Rebuttal (RB): 'however', 'although', 'on the contrary'” 

Inference indicators 

Penanda linguistik yang menunjukkan bahwa penulis sedang menarik kesimpulan atau inferensi dari informasi sebelumnya. 

“Main Claim (MC): 'therefore', 'thus', 'in conclusion'" dan "Warrant (WR): 'this means', 'which implies', 'suggesting that” 

Paragraph transitions 

Frasa atau ungkapan yang menandai perpindahan antar paragraf dan menjaga alur logis antar bagian tulisan. 

“Discourse often relies on implicit logical connections without explicit markers" dan "CNN excels at identifying argumentative connectives... such as transitional phrases ('therefore', 'however', 'firstly...secondly')” 

Cohesion chains 

Rangkaian elemen teks yang saling terhubung dan menjaga keutuhan wacana, diukur dari frekuensi dan konsistensi penggunaan penghubung logis. 

“The language feature correlation matrix shows a correlation coefficient of 0.81 between argument density and connective usage frequency, confirming the crucial role of logical connectives in constructing argumentation networks.” 

Reference resolution 

Kemampuan mengidentifikasi rujukan kata ganti (pronoun) atau anafora dalam teks — menentukan apa yang dimaksud oleh kata seperti "this", "it", atau "its". 

Reference disambiguation errors (24.3%) reflect the challenges posed by Chinese pronouns and zero anaphora phenomena, particularly when pronouns like 'this' or 'its' refer to complex propositional content. 

Vocabulary diversity 

Keragaman kosakata yang digunakan dalam tulisan, diukur melalui Type-Token Ratio (TTR) — rasio antara jumlah kata unik terhadap total kata. 

“Grade 9 students achieved a vocabulary of 5218 unique words, representing a 35.6% increase from Grade 7... directly reflected in the type–token ratio (TTR) increasing from 0.42 to 0.53.” 

Academic words 

Frekuensi penggunaan kosakata akademik dalam tulisan — semakin tinggi penggunaannya, semakin matang kemampuan berpikir kritis siswa. 

“academic vocabulary usage frequency shows a positive correlation with critical thinking maturity, providing actionable guidance for teaching practice.”
